Governance Overview

Nipana is a prominent village in the Khamgaon Taluka of Buldhana District. Currently, the village is governed by an **Administrative System** to ensure continuity in development and public service delivery.

The administration works closely with the residents to manage infrastructure projects, social welfare schemes, and maintain law and order within the village limits.
